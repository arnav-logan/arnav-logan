Section 2

Question 1: What do you notice about the relationship between the first-half (top four rows) of the full-adder as compared to all of the rows of the half-adder?

They are the same.

Question 2: Why is this true?

Because the first four rows don't require a carry to be added so they will work on both adders.

Because it will not have a carry due to being the first digit.
